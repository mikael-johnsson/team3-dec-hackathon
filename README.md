The **Goal** section provides a concise summary of the main objective or purpose of the project described in this README. It addresses the following aspects:  

- ğŸ„ **Problem Statement:** <br> When you think of an original advent calendar, you think of the one you purchase from your local shops and retail stores. You very rarely see a online, **interactive** advent calendar.   
- ğŸ„ **Objective(s):** <br> Users can interact with the calendar day to day, with each day allowing the user to interact with a new door. Each door provides it own **unique** content. Leading to a new <em>fun fact, riddle, joke or image related to Christmas</em>. The aim is to allow users to have fun while also counting down the days to Christmas.  
- ğŸ„ **Target Audience:** <br> Anyone who would like to experience a unique way to enjoy the countdown to Christmas and may not of had a chance to purchase a traditional advent calender. Just pop online and enjoy.
